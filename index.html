About Us

GTS TRAILERS AFRICA LTD is a duly registered limited liability company in Ghana, established as a branch of GT GURLESENYIL TRAILER from Ankara-Turkey. With exports to more than 70 countries worldwide, we hold the prestigious "European type approval certificate" for high performance.

Led by our Founder & CEO, Mr. John Baidoo, who brings over 25 years of professional experience from the United States as an ASE-certified Master Technician and Operations Specialist, we deliver exceptional quality and service.
