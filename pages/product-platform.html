Flatbed Trailers

Versatile platform semi-trailers for general cargo transport

Flatbed Semi Trailers (Without Cover, Platform, Container, step frame trailer) with 2, 3 or 4 axles or with single or double tires according to the capacity.

Technical Highlights

Our flatbed trailers come in several variants to meet your specific transportation needs:

  • Flatbed Semi Trailers (Without Cover, Platform, Container, step frame trailer)
  • Flatbed semi-trailers with cover (Usually for 13600 mm length, general use)
  • B-link Semi-trailers (used as a train)
  • Semi-trailers used for forest wood log transport
  • Platform semi-trailers that can be extended up to 45 meters (Used to transport long solid loads)
  • Available with container locks for container transport
  • Options for 2, 3 or 4 axles with single or double tires based on capacity requirements
  • Available with air suspension or steel suspension

Additional Features

  • Semi-trailers that can be extended by 2 or 3 steps with hydraulic steering axles
  • Competitive pricing with premium quality construction
  • Super link and B-link options available for various products including cement silo, tipper semi-trailer, and container transport
