Company Background

GTS TRAILERS AFRICA LTD is a duly registered limited liability company in Ghana, established as a branch of GT GURLESENYIL TRAILER from Ankara-Turkey.

Our parent company has a distinguished track record in the heavy cargo transportation sector, exporting high-quality trailers to more than 70 countries worldwide. The Ghana branch was established to serve the growing needs of the African market, providing European-standard trailer products and services locally.

Our operations are backed by the prestigious "European type approval certificate" for high performance, ensuring that all our products meet rigorous international standards.
