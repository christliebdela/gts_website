Company Background

GTS TRAILERS AFRICA LTD is a duly registered limited liability company in Ghana, established as a branch of GT GURLESENYIL TRAILER from Ankara-Turkey.

Our parent company has a distinguished track record in the heavy cargo transportation sector, exporting high-quality trailers to more than 70 countries worldwide. The Ghana branch was established to serve the growing needs of the African market, providing European-standard trailer products and services locally.

Our operations are backed by the prestigious "European type approval certificate" for high performance, ensuring that all our products meet rigorous international standards.

Our Mission

To provide quality and safe vehicles for customers in Ghana and Africa by combining experiences with strong production infrastructure in the heavy cargo transportation sector, understanding customer needs and expectations in the best way.

At GTS TRAILERS AFRICA LTD, we are dedicated to delivering superior products that meet the highest safety standards while understanding the unique requirements of the African market.

Our Vision

To follow technological developments with the aim of reaching better production standards, providing reliable and consistent quality services worldwide, and becoming a world brand that provides solutions to customers in all conditions. To become the one-stop-shop for all best quality world standard semitrailer production and supply within Ghana and the rest of Africa.

We continuously strive to integrate the latest technologies and innovations to maintain our position as the premier trailer manufacturer in the region.
