GTS Trailers Africa Announces Major Expansion into East African Markets

GTS Trailers Africa Ltd is proud to announce a significant expansion of our operations into East African markets, marking a pivotal moment in our company's growth strategy. This expansion will enable us to better serve the transportation needs of businesses throughout the region with our high-quality trailer solutions.

The expansion includes establishing new manufacturing facilities and distribution centers in strategic locations across East Africa, creating jobs and fostering economic development in the regions where we operate.

"This expansion represents our commitment to serving the African continent with locally manufactured, high-quality transportation solutions," said the CEO of GTS Trailers Africa Ltd. "We're excited about the opportunities this presents for our company, our employees, and our customers."

August 1, 2025 Product Launch

GTS TRAILERS AFRICA LTD Launches New Line of Eco-Friendly Fuel Tankers

GTS TRAILERS AFRICA LTD is proud to announce the launch of our new line of eco-friendly fuel tankers designed to meet the growing demands of the petroleum transportation industry while reducing environmental impact.

These new tankers feature advanced materials that reduce overall weight while maintaining strength, resulting in improved fuel efficiency for the towing vehicle. Additionally, innovative safety features have been incorporated to prevent leaks and spills during transport.

"This new line represents our commitment to sustainable practices while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety that our clients expect," said Mr. John Baidoo, Founder & CEO of GTS TRAILERS AFRICA LTD. "We believe these eco-friendly tankers will set a new standard in the industry."

The new tankers are available for order immediately, with demonstrations available at our Tema facility.
