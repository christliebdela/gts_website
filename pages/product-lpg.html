Pressure-Rated LPG Transport Excellence

Engineered LPG bulk tanker semi‑trailers delivering certified safety, optimized payload and reliable discharge performance for regional gas distribution and bulk energy logistics.

Available capacities from 40 m³ to 75 m³ with 2–5 axle configurations depending on regulatory weight limits and route topology. Each vessel undergoes stringent TUV Austria hydrostatic testing and non‑destructive examination to ensure structural integrity and safety margin compliance.

Key Technical Highlights

  • Capacities: 40 / 45 / 50 / 55 / 60 / 65 / 70 / 75 m³
  • Design codes: International pressure vessel standards & regional compliance
  • Inspection: Hydrostatic + radiographic / ultrasonic NDT (TUV witnessed)
  • Materials: Fine‑grain high strength pressure vessel steel
  • Corrosion protection: Internal cleanliness & multi‑layer exterior coating
  • Valving: Emergency shut off, pressure relief & excess flow devices
  • Discharge: Liquid & vapour phase service connections (bottom manifold)
  • Safety: Excess flow valves, rollover protection, earthing & static bonding
  • Options: Flow meters, digital level gauging, anti‑roll EBS stability

Operational & Safety Features

  • Certified pressure relief valve assemblies
  • Fire‑resistant valve cabinet layout with ergonomic access
  • Anti‑static earthing points & grounding cable reels
  • High visibility ADR compliant lighting & conspicuity markings
  • Emergency remote shutoff readiness (option)
  • Walkway & manhole access with anti‑slip profile (option)
