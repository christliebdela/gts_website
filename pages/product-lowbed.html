Low Loader / Lowbed Trailers

Heavy haul solutions 20–150t • 2–10 axle configurable • Extendable & Gooseneck

  1. Home
  2. Products
  3. Low Loader / Lowbed

Low loader semi-trailers from 2 axles to 8 axles, from 20 ton capacity to 150 tons carrying capacity. Options include hydraulic or self-steering axles, pool recess, extendable chassis, hydraulic side expansion, front loading, folding gooseneck lowbeds, or custom special designs.

We use specially produced rubber on the platform providing longevity, non-slip performance, practicality, aesthetic appearance and environmental friendliness. The GTS Semitrailer low loader series is engineered for demanding heavy transport with 2 to 10 axles (standard range 2–8) and width configurations from 2550–4000 mm, equipped with hydraulic steering and pneumatic or hydraulic suspension. With special chassis technology and robust structure, GTS low-loaders ensure safe transportation of heavy and special goods.

Technical Highlights

  • Payload classes from 20t up to 150t
  • Axle configurations 2–10 (hydraulic or self-steering)
  • Extendable chassis & hydraulic side expansion options
  • Pool / recessed deck and drop well variants
  • Front loading and folding gooseneck mechanisms
  • Custom special design engineering for unique cargo
  • High-durability, eco-friendly non-slip rubber deck surface
  • Width range 2550–4000 mm with expansion modules
  • Hydraulic steering with pneumatic or hydraulic suspension

Operational & Safety Features

  • Optimized chassis stress distribution for point & distributed loads
  • Modular ramp & gooseneck assemblies for rapid reconfiguration
  • High visibility & ADR compliant lighting (option)
  • Multiple lashing, stake, expansion & twist-lock points
  • Corrosion protection: shot blast + multi-layer industrial coating
  • Telematics / load monitoring sensor readiness (optional)
