Concrete Mixer Semi-Trailers Overview

Robust, high‑capacity concrete mixer semi‑trailers engineered for reliable transport and homogeneous mixing of ready‑mix concrete across challenging African haul routes. Built for durability, stability and efficient discharge.

Configurable with 2, 3 or 4 axles and drum capacities of 12 / 14 / 15 / 16 m³. Drive options include PTO (Power Take Off) from the tractor or an independent diesel engine power pack to match site operating conditions.

Key Technical Highlights

  • Drum capacities: 12 / 14 / 15 / 16 m³
  • Axle configurations: 2, 3 or 4 axles (mechanical or air suspension)
  • Drive types: PTO or auxiliary diesel engine module
  • Optimized spiral blade geometry for homogeneous mixing & rapid discharge
  • High wear resistant drum steel & optional internal liner for extended service life
  • Low center of gravity chassis design improves stability & safety
  • Integrated water tank & spray / cleaning system
  • Hydraulic drive with planetary reduction gearbox & robust support bearings
  • Anti‑slip rear access ladder & service platform with safety rail
  • Multiple chute extensions with easy stow brackets
