Container Sidelifter Semi Trailer Overview

The side loader (sidelifter) is a self‑loading semi trailer system that lifts and transfers containers laterally, enabling fast, independent handling without cranes or reach stackers. Ideal for depots, ports, logistics yards and customer sites where space and turnaround time are critical.

Built with high strength ST quality steel and engineered hydraulic lift modules, GT's sidelifter semi trailers deliver reliable 45 ton load / lift capacity while accommodating 20, 30, 40 ft (optional 45 ft) ISO containers. Professional, safe and efficient container logistics – anywhere.

Core Features

  • 45 ton loading & transport capacity (application dependent)
  • Compatible with 20', 30', 40' (optional 45') ISO containers
  • Self‑loading hydraulic sidelifter cranes with lateral precision
  • SAF, BPW or certified Turkish axle options
  • Self‑steering axle configurations available
  • Single or double tyre setups; steel or aluminium wheels
  • Mechanical (leaf) or air suspension choices
  • ST quality high strength steel chassis & lift frame
  • EBS brake system with stability support
  • EU standard twist locks (8 standard, up to 10 on extendable chassis)

Chassis & Configuration Options

  • Skeleton Container Semi Trailer: Multi‑position twist locks for 20 / 30 / 40 / 45 ft; 8 EU standard locks.
  • Dump Chassis Container Semi Trailer: Tipping capability for varied payload volumes – chassis & platform adaptable.
  • Extendable / High Cube Chassis: Telescopic frame; up to 10 EU standard locks; optimized for long & high cube containers.

Long Life Value

GT Semitrailer designs match customer duty cycles – delivering durability, low maintenance cost and operational flexibility. Quality materials and controlled fabrication extend lifecycle and residual value.
