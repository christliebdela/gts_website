Cement Bulk (V / W Type)

Dry bulk silo trailers for powder, cement & granular materials

  1. Home
  2. Products
  3. Cement Bulk

Cement or Flour Silo – Dry Bulk semi-trailers for transporting cement, flour or granular product in V Type or W Type (Dry Bulk) bodies. Bodies available in black sheet (carbon steel) or lightweight aluminium construction. Compressor drive options include electric motor or diesel engine packages to match site infrastructure and operational autonomy.

Technical Highlights

  • Body configurations: V Type or W Type dry bulk geometries
  • Material options: Carbon (black) steel or aluminium body
  • Product range: Cement, flour, fly ash & granular powders
  • Compressor drive: Electric motor or diesel engine
  • Optimized cone angles for fast fluidization & discharge
  • Low residual material design – smooth internal surfaces

Operational & Safety Features

  • High-flow aeration system for uniform fluidization
  • Pressure relief & safety valves compliant with standards
  • Accessible top manholes for inspection & cleaning
  • Anti-corrosion coating / anodized aluminium finish
  • Optional load cells & discharge metering integration
