Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for all your trailer and heavy equipment needs

Manufacturing All Kinds of Trailers

At GTS TRAILERS AFRICA LTD, we specialize in the design and manufacturing of various types of trailers to meet diverse transportation needs. Our manufacturing capabilities include:

  • Flatbed Trailers
  • Fuel Tanker Semitrailers
  • LPG Bulk Tankers
  • Low Loaders/Lowbeds
  • Tipper Dump Trailers
  • Cement Bulk Trailers
  • Concrete Mixer Semitrailers

All our trailers are built to European standards, ensuring durability, safety, and optimal performance.

General Metal Fabrication and Welding Works

Our state-of-the-art metal fabrication workshop offers comprehensive solutions for various metal fabrication needs, including:

  • Precision metal cutting and shaping
  • High-quality welding services
  • Custom metal parts fabrication
  • Sheet metal work
  • Structural steel fabrication
  • Metal bending and forming

We work with different metals and employ advanced techniques to deliver superior results for both small and large-scale projects.

Trailers Refurbishment & Repairs

Extend the life and improve the performance of your existing trailers with our comprehensive refurbishment and repair services:

  • Complete structural repairs
  • Hydraulic system overhaul
  • Brake system servicing
  • Suspension repairs and upgrades
  • Electrical system diagnostics and repairs
  • Body repair and repainting
  • Interior upgrades and modifications

Our experienced technicians can restore your trailer to optimal condition, regardless of its current state.

Truck Repairs & Maintenance Services

Keep your trucks operating at peak performance with our comprehensive repair and maintenance services:

  • Engine diagnostics and repairs
  • Transmission servicing
  • Brake system maintenance
  • Electrical system repairs
  • Suspension and steering system maintenance
  • Preventive maintenance programs
  • Emergency repair services

Our team includes ASE-certified technicians with extensive experience working on various truck makes and models.

Light & Heavy Vehicle Wheel Alignment Services

Proper wheel alignment is crucial for vehicle safety, tire longevity, and fuel efficiency. Our alignment services include:

  • Computerized wheel alignment for light vehicles
  • Specialized alignment for heavy-duty trucks and trailers
  • Multi-axle alignment services
  • Camber, caster, and toe adjustment
  • Tire balancing
  • Suspension inspection

We use advanced alignment equipment to ensure precise adjustments for optimal vehicle performance.

  • Electrical components
  • Trailer accessories
  • Filters, fluids, and lubricants

Our parts inventory includes both OEM and high-quality aftermarket options to suit different budgets while maintaining quality standards.
