Tipper Dump Trailers

U, Box, Scrap, Side Tipping & Insulated Hardox HB450 Variants

  1. Home
  2. Products
  3. Tipper Dump Trailers

Our tipper semi-trailers are available in multiple engineered body variants to match diverse material transport requirements, each optimized for durability, discharge efficiency and payload protection.

Technical Highlights

  • U Type Tipper – Generally 24 m³ for excavation; base 4 mm & sides 4 mm in HB 450 Hardox (or equivalent). Optional hydraulic rear cover.
  • Box Tipper – General purpose box form with side supports for broad aggregate & bulk usage.
  • Scrap Dump – Accordion-shaped reinforced side covers; HB 450 wear steel for lighter tare and high abrasion resistance in scrap handling.
  • Side Tipping Dump – Lateral discharge to either side (model dependent) for confined or sectional unloading zones.
  • U Type Insulated Tipper – 100 mm stone wool external insulation layer to preserve hot asphalt temperature; sides 4 mm HB 450; hydraulic rear cover; compatible with automatic tarping system.

Operational & Safety Features

  • High wear resistance HB 450 Hardox (or equivalent) for extended service life
  • Hydraulic cylinder configurations sized for rapid full lift cycles
  • Optional hydraulic or pneumatic tailgate actuation
  • Load covering: manual or automatic tarp systems (insulated models standard automated)
  • Optimized body geometry for clean material ejection & reduced hang-up
  • Integrated ladder, grab points & LED lighting (ADR option)
